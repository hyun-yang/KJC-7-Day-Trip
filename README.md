## Inspiration

Planning a trip to East Asia is exciting — but the language barrier can be daunting. Most phrasebooks are generic and don't account for *where* you're actually going or *what* you'll actually be doing there. I built KJC 7-Day Trip so travelers can learn the exact expressions they'll need, tailored to their specific destination and activities, in the days leading up to their trip.

## What It Does

KJC 7-Day Trip generates context-aware, essential phrases powered by OpenAI's language model. The flow is simple:

1. **Pick your country** — Korea, Japan, or China
2. **Choose a city** — select the city you're visiting
3. **Select places** — pick the specific spots you plan to go (restaurants, train stations, tourist sites, etc.)
4. **Choose a learning category** — greetings, ordering food, asking for directions, emergency phrases, and more

Based on that context, the app generates practical, real-world expressions so you can practice and get comfortable with the language before and during your trip.

## Key Features

- **Context-aware phrase generation** — phrases are tailored to your destination, not generic textbook sentences
- **City and place-specific content** — learn what you'll actually need at the places you're visiting
- **Multiple learning categories** — from basic greetings to ordering food to emergency situations
- **Powered by GPT-5.6** — leverages OpenAI's model for natural, up-to-date expressions
- **Practice-friendly format** — designed for quick study sessions in the days before your trip

## Usage

1. Launch the app
2. Select your destination country (Korea, Japan, or China)
3. Choose the city you're visiting
4. Pick the places you plan to go
5. Select a learning category
6. Study the generated phrases and practice before your trip!
